Californicorns

New Album Out Now

Original and throwback. Shredded gnar and sultry croon. The Californicorns blend classic American genres (Surf. Soul. Funk. Rock & Roll.) with a touch of contemporary nonsense. Their compositions are eclectic, climbing a wall of sound to a sweaty dance floor filled with sentimental ballads and get-your-ass-off-the-wall jams.
